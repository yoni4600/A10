Lesson 1: Basics of Spanish

Introduction to Spanish

Spanish is a rich language with a long history and is spoken by millions around the world. In this lesson, we'll cover some basic phrases and introductions to get you started.

Basic Phrases

  • Hello: Hola
  • Goodbye: Adiós
  • Please: Por favor
  • Thank you: Gracias
  • Yes: Sí
  • No: No
