Lesson 4: Spanish Vocabulary

Expanding Your Spanish Vocabulary

A rich vocabulary is essential for fluent communication. Here are some common categories to focus on:

Colors

  • Red: Rojo
  • Blue: Azul
  • Green: Verde

Family

  • Mother: Madre
  • Father: Padre
  • Sibling: Hermano/Hermana

Try to use these new words in sentences to practice their usage.
